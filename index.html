Crinck Associates Limited

Date

2023

Client

Crinck Associates
Limited

Crinck Associates Limited is an architectural firm that required a professional website to display their portfolio, introduce their team, and allow clients to reach out easily. The website consists of a Home, About, Projects, and Contact Us page, providing visitors with a seamless browsing experience. Additionally, an admin panel was included to manage team members, projects, and contact messages efficiently.

The challenge

One of the key challenges was ensuring that the firm could easily update their project portfolio and team members without requiring technical expertise. Another challenge was managing contact form submissions effectively so that inquiries were not lost or overlooked. The website needed a robust yet simple solution to handle these tasks efficiently.

My solution

To address these challenges, I built an admin dashboard using PHP and MySQL, allowing authorized users to perform CRUD operations on team members and projects. The contact form submissions were also integrated into the dashboard for easy management. The frontend, developed with HTML and CSS, ensures a visually appealing and responsive user experience. This solution provided Crinck Associates Limited with a functional, easily manageable website that enhances their online presence and operational efficiency.
